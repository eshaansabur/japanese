Aptis tests English skills relevant to your requirements. It offers you the flexibility to either assess all four English skills – reading, writing, listening and speaking together with the core mandatory component (grammar and vocabulary) or test just one skill, e.g. writing.

There are four parts to the Aptis writing test. You will fill in forms, interact in a social media-type written conversation and write emails.

All writing tasks are marked by an examiner. The writing test takes up to 55 minutes, the timings for each section below are recommendations only.
